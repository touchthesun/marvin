Understanding Neo4j Graph Databases

Graph databases are becoming increasingly popular for managing complex, connected data.

Why Neo4j?

Neo4j is one of the leading graph database platforms, offering:

  • Native graph storage and processing
  • Cypher query language
  • ACID transactions
  • High availability clustering

Key Concepts

Understanding these key concepts is essential:

  • Nodes: Entities in your domain
  • Relationships: Connections between nodes
  • Properties: Attributes of nodes and relationships
  • Labels: Grouping and categorization
